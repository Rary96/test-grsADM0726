Methodology and caveats

Average and benchmark. The average is pooled across all responses from all parks and is fixed, slicers never move it. The range shows the lowest and highest individual park, kept anonymous. In a park view the slicer applies to that park only, the average and the band hold still.

Indexing. A segment over or under indexes when its mean differs from the current view's average at p < 0.05 on a Welch two-sample t-test. NPS is scored at respondent level (+100 promoter, 0 passive, -100 detractor). Segments under n=30, and combinations under n=40, are not tested.
